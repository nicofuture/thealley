A Little History

Located in Miami, Florida, Little River is a historic neighborhood known for its diverse culture, unique architecture, and flourishing art scene. Once a trading post in the 19th century, Little River offers a mix of boutiques, eclectic eateries, and vibrant community events, making it a distinctive spot in the heart of Miami.

Culinary Landscape

Local dining in Little River offers a culinary journey that mirrors the area's rich cultural tapestry. From trendy cafes serving artisanal brews to eateries dishing out global flavors, the restaurant scene in Little River is a testament to the neighborhood's vibrant and evolving character. Whether you're seeking avant-garde cuisine or comforting homestyle dishes, Little River promises a delightful gastronomic adventure.

Cultural Renaissance

Little River is becoming the epicenter of artistic evolution, drawing attention for its burgeoning art scene. Newly established art havens, such as Mindy Solomon gallery, Oolite Arts, and Fountainhead Studios are quickly transforming the neighborhood's aesthetic landscape.
